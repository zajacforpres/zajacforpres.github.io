I am Alec Zajac

My name is Alec Zajac and I am running for president of the United States of America in 2020. I am currently in college and I had a lot of extra time on my hands, so I figured, why not run for president?

My fellow Americans, we are at a time in our nation’s history where many view politics as nothing more than a joke. An important joke, perhaps, but a joke nonetheless. So, I think it is important not to deny this, but instead to embrace it. And who better to do that than an unqualified imbecile like myself?

My Mission

“My mission is to meme it up in the presidency. As a side gig, I’d also like to get rid of unemployment. We’ll see about that second one. But most of all, I am here to be a politician, which means pretending to be for the people, while actually furthering my self-interests.”
