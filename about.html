I am Alec Zajac

Hello, my name is Alec Zajac and I am running for president in 2020. Many of you are probably on this page because you want to know who I am and why I am running for president.

As for who I am, I'm a very bored college student with too much free time on my hands. This tradition of having too much time on my hands goes all the way back to my high school years, when I first decided to enter into politics. I ran in the 2016 presidential election as an independent and while I'm not sure exactly how many votes I received, my estimates put me at anywhere between 3 and 760,210.

One question many people have for me is if I am allowed to run, since I am under 35 years old. To that I would suggest that they check their facts. It is a common misconception that, in America, someone must be 35 years old to run for president. In fact, you must be 35 to win the presidency. So why am I running if I can't win? To build a base of support that will help me once I am old enough to win, of course.

I feel that many of the issues I address resonate with many people, but these issues are ones I have not seen even mentioned in passing by any big politician. I am here to be a champion for these underrepresented views. So why not vote for me? What's the worst that could happen?
